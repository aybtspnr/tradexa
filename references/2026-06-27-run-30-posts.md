## Lições Aprendidas

- Direct-to-project-clean workflow funciona bem — backups de shared files entre batches essenciais
- `getAllPosts` missing in `posts.ts` is a pre-existing bug that blocks ALL deploys — fix is simple and should be committed
- 3 subagents × 2 posts = ~4 min cada batch (médio 425s batch 3 foi o mais lento devido a correções de formatação)
- deepseek-v4-flash consistently produces 2500-4700 word posts — very reliable
- Post-deploy verification confirmed all new content present on live site (29-35KB per post, well above 12KB template baseline)
- Known issues: "Article" schema missing (cronológico), OG title truncation with "..." (makeTitle not deployed)
